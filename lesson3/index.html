Ogden City Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Ogden City Chamber of Commerce page will be a resource to all the new and existing businesses of Ogden, Utah. It will provide resources for businesses, such as the ability to connect and network with other owners. Another goal of the site is to help businesses develop strong connections with the public, advertise information, and create exposure. It will benefit those who live and shop in the community and will help it grow.

Target Audience

Personas

Our target audience is the businesses owners of Ogden and the residents who shop and support those businesses.

Persona 1 just graduated from Weber State University and wants to create a new local business. She wants help with knowing how to get started and to connect with other local businesses.

Personal 2 is a stay at home dad and wants to know about any deals or events that might be occurring. He would like to find ways to support those in his community.

Scenarios

  1. What licensing and/or registration do I need to start a business in Ogden?
  2. What events are occurring and when? (ex. When’s the farmers market?)
  3. How do I network with other businesses around me?
  4. What are the top 10 rated business I should visit if I’m new to the area?
  5. If I am planning an event, what businesses can I use for resources?
